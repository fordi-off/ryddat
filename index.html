The problem

Most companies still manage supplier documentation through email threads, shared drives, and spreadsheets. It works — until it doesn't.

Nobody knows what's missing

There's no reliable overview of which suppliers have complete documentation and which don't.

Documents go stale

Certificates and declarations expire quietly, and outdated documents keep being used.

Follow-up is manual

Employees have to remember who they emailed, when, and whether anyone ever replied.

Same request, twice

Without a shared system, different employees end up asking the same supplier for the same file.

Knowledge walks out the door

When an employee leaves, nobody knows what was requested, received, or still outstanding.

It's slow, not hard

Each request is simple. Doing it across dozens or hundreds of suppliers adds up fast.
