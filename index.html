The problem

Nothing here is hard. It's just scattered.

Most companies still track supplier documentation through email threads, shared drives, and spreadsheets. Each request is simple on its own — doing it across dozens or hundreds of suppliers is what breaks down.

Nobody knows what's missing

There's no reliable overview of which suppliers have complete documentation and which don't.

Documents go stale

Certificates and declarations expire quietly, and outdated documents keep getting used.

Same request, twice

Without a shared system, different employees end up asking one supplier for the same file.

Follow-up is manual

Employees have to remember who they emailed, when, and whether anyone replied.

Knowledge walks out the door

When an employee leaves, nobody knows what was requested, received, or still outstanding.

It's slow, not hard

Each request takes minutes. Doing it across hundreds of suppliers adds up to real hours every week.
