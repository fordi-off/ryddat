The problem

Nothing about this is hard. It's just scattered.

Most companies still track supplier documentation through email threads, shared drives, and spreadsheets. Each request is simple — doing it across dozens or hundreds of suppliers is what breaks down.

  1. 01

    Nobody knows what's missing

    There's no reliable overview of which suppliers have complete documentation and which don't.

  2. 02

    Documents go stale

    Certificates and declarations expire quietly, and outdated documents keep getting used.

  3. 03

    Follow-up is manual

    Employees have to remember who they emailed, when, and whether anyone replied.

  4. 04

    Same request, twice

    Without a shared system, different employees end up asking one supplier for the same file.

  5. 05

    Knowledge walks out the door

    When an employee leaves, nobody knows what was requested, received, or still outstanding.

How it works

One workflow, start to finish.

  1. 01

    Add suppliers & requirements

    Build your supplier list and define which documents each one needs to provide.

  2. 02

    See what's missing

    One view shows missing, expiring, and expired documents across every supplier.

  3. 03

    Request & track

    Generate a request email, send it, and track the reply — with reminders when suppliers go quiet.

  4. 04

    Store & monitor

    Received files land in the right supplier profile, expiration dates tracked automatically.
